Divya
Founder

Divya, a trailblazing force in the realm of marketing, holds an MBA in Marketing from Mahatma Gandhi University, India, laying a sturdy foundation for her illustrious career. Prior to this, she honed her skills with a Bachelor's in Business Administration from the University of Madras, India, solidifying her expertise in the field.
Currently, Divya is fervently pursuing her passion for research by undertaking a PhD in Marketing at the University of Bolton. Despite facing setbacks, such as the disruption caused by the pandemic leading to the temporary suspension of her studies at the RAK Campus in 2019, Divya remains undeterred in her pursuit of academic excellence.
With a relentless drive for knowledge and innovation, Divya is poised to make significant contributions to the marketing landscape, armed with a potent combination of academic prowess and real-world experience. Her unwavering commitment to her field is emblematic of her determination to leave an indelible mark on the industry.

JABIR SIDHEEK
Pilot trainer

Born in 1991 in Puthanpalli, Malappuram, I entered this world as the youngest among four siblings to Mr. T.C. Kunju and Beevathutty. At 19, my life took a tumultuous turn during my final year of commerce studies. A severe accident crushed 30% of my left leg below the knee, shattering flesh, nerves, and bones. Though divine grace and skillful surgery saved my limb, my small toe was lost in the ordeal. Confined to bed for a year, I faced an immense challenge with unwavering determination. Slowly but surely, I relearned to walk, aided by a cane. This experience forged me into a motivational trainer, granting me a second birth in the crucible of adversity.
Today, I stand as a renowned infotainment specialist, guiding students and parents alike, and wielding influence across social media with millions of followers. With over 1400 live training programs and 350 online sessions under my belt, I am a living testament to resilience, empowering countless individuals to overcome their obstacles and achieve greatness.
